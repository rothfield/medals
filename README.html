Medal Count Module

This Module shows up to 10 countries that have won the most medals of a given kind in the Olympics games. It has the following requirements: -It can accept up to two parameters:

  • element_id - an id of the element that the module will use as its container.
  • sort -­‐ determines the type of medals to sort by. It can have one of the following values: 'total', 'gold', 'silver' and 'bronze'.

-If no sort parameter is passed the sort should be by gold medals.

-When ranking by total medals, ties are broken by most gold.

-When ranking by gold, ties are broken by most silver. -When ranking by silver, ties are broken by most gold. -When ranking by bronze, ties are broken by most gold.

-Users can click the column headers to re-­‐sort the countries by gold, silver, bronze, or total medals won. With each sort the possibility exists that new countries will enter and depart the top 10.

-To get the list of countries and their medals use the following URL: https://s3-­‐us-­‐west-­‐ 2.amazonaws.com/reuters.medals-­‐widget/medals.json
